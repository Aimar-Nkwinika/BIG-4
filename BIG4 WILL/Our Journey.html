Our Journey

Take a look at the steps we’ve taken to turn this project into a reality, one milestone at a time.

Project Kickoff

26 July 2024 - We initiated the project with the goal of designing a sustainable network infrastructure for the uThukela District's main hospital.

Initial Research and Planning

August 2024 - Our team conducted detailed research into the existing infrastructure, local requirements, and Openserve’s capabilities as the recommended carrier.

Infrastructure Design Finalized

September 2024 - The final network infrastructure design was completed, including security measures, hardware recommendations, and data management solutions.

Implementation Begins

October 2024 - Installation of necessary hardware and extension of the network to the hospital began, with a focus on ensuring a stable connection and addressing outdated systems.

Testing and Troubleshooting

October 2024 - We performed extensive testing to ensure the network was secure, efficient, and met the hospital's needs. Several rounds of troubleshooting were carried out.

Project Completion

02 November 2024 - The infrastructure was successfully installed, tested, and delivered, creating a fast, secure, and reliable network for the uThukela District’s main hospital.
