Our Final Work

After months of planning, research, and development, we are proud to present the final outcomes of the uThukela Network Infrastructure Project.

Network Infrastructure

We designed and implemented a world-class, secure, and reliable network infrastructure tailored to meet the needs of the uThukela District’s main hospital.

Security Measures

Our team ensured that all necessary security measures were put in place to safeguard the hospital's network from potential cyber threats.

Hardware and Software Upgrades

We replaced outdated equipment with modern computers, printers, and other essential devices to improve productivity.

Data Management System

We implemented an efficient data management system to address the hospital's critical issue of missing patient files and enhance file storage and retrieval.

Network Expansion

We extended the existing network to cover all 45 wards and 5 offices, ensuring that all areas of the hospital have access to high-speed internet.
